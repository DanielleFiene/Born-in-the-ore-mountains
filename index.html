Breeding Top Quality Flat-Coated Retrievers For 15 Years

Born in the Ore Mountains is a renowned breeding station specializing in Flat-Coated Retrievers.

Nestled in the picturesque Ore Mountains, this breeding station is dedicated to producing high-quality,
healthy, and well-tempered Flat-Coated Retrievers.

We, at Born in the Ore Mountains, emphasize careful selection, ensuring our dogs possess excellent genetic traits,
optimal health, and strong working abilities.

Puppies are raised in a loving environment, with early socialization and training, preparing them for various roles, from family companions to skilled working dogs.

This commitment to excellence has earned Born in the Ore Mountains a stellar reputation among Flat-Coated Retriever enthusiasts.
